## Becoming a Maintainer

We welcome contributors from the community to join the maintainer team! Criteria for becoming a maintainer include:

1.  **Consistent Contributions**: A history of high-quality contributions (code, docs, or community support).
2.  **Project Understanding**: Demonstrated deep understanding of the Flinkflow architecture and the Flink ecosystem.
3.  **Community Leadership**: Helping other contributors and participating in design discussions.

If you are interested in becoming a maintainer, please reach out to the existing maintainer team at **contact@talweg.ai**.
